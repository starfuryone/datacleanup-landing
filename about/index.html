Our Story

DataCleanup Pro was founded by data engineers who experienced the pain of messy spreadsheets firsthand. After spending countless hours manually cleaning customer databases, lead lists, and inventory files, we knew there had to be a better way.

2023
Founded DataCleanup Pro after experiencing the frustration of manual data cleaning in our consulting work. Built the first version of our AI-powered cleaning algorithms.
2024
Launched our API service and processed our first 10,000 rows of customer data. Added support for Excel files and AI-powered categorization.
2025
Expanded to serve 500+ businesses with our professional data cleaning service. Introduced advanced deduplication and comprehensive PDF reporting.
